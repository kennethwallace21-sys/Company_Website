Approved Positioning
CAAi Command Center is an operations intelligence layer for multi-unit operators. It turns live store data into daily priorities, alerts, and grounded answers for owners and managers.
CAAi Command Center

Daily manager briefings, cross-store visibility, and grounded answers in one operating surface.

  • Morning briefings surface the top actions for the day instead of forcing managers to hunt for context.
  • Owner-level visibility shows labor, sales, inventory, and operational exceptions across all stores.
  • Alerts focus managers on what needs intervention now: waste, staffing risk, service issues, and execution gaps.
  • Grounded Q&A sits on top of actual store materials and operating data, not generic assistant behavior.
Sample Daily Briefing
Labor watch Store 104 is trending above plan for the morning rush. Adjust staffing against sales per labor hour before 10:30 AM.
Waste risk Breakfast sandwich pull is running hot at one location. Check pars before the second prep window.
Service execution Drive-thru delay patterns point to one training gap on handoff and loyalty scanning.
Owner view All stores can be reviewed in one place without another spreadsheet pass.
Why The RAG Matters

The stack wins because the answer path is grounded, verified, and already running locally.

Current Live RAG Stack
Ingest

snowflake-arctic-embed2:l is the live embedding baseline for the current local-first posture.
Infer
Nemotron 3 Nano 30B-A3B runs locally through Ollama on the RTX 5090.
Verify
CAAi Trust Layer screens prompts, validates grounding, checks citations, and emits verdicts.
Export
Requests, responses, verdicts, and evidence can be reviewed or exported for audit-friendly follow-up.
